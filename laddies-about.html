Our Heritage

Weaving Tradition into Modernity

Welcome to Noor-e-Libas, a boutique that stands at the intersection of timeless Pakistani tradition and contemporary fashion. Established in the heart of Lahore, our journey began with a singular vision: to revive the lost art of hand-embroidery and present it to the modern woman.

Each piece in our collection is not just a dress, but a labor of love. We collaborate with skilled artisans from rural Punjab and Sindh, ensuring that every stitch tells a story of culture, dedication, and artistry.
